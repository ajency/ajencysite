Minyawns.com
A job listing site where employers can hire University students for small tasks.

There are dozens of small job listing sites including market leaders like TaskRabbit.com and Fancyhands.com. Minyawns.com (a take on the word Minion) differentiates itself by focusing on University students only. Started by a student of University of Washington, it has started out for the Seattle region and is seeing healthy adoption.

Landing page - Conversions

When we set out to design the home page, we had a clear brief - Get as many conversions (signups) as possible.

A mix of A/B testing and good design practices ensured a healthy sign up success for the home page. Being a responsive site, even the mobile signups were a significant percentage.

 

 

Browse Jobs & Minions

It's funny how a directory or listing of jobs and employees has been done a few hundred thousand times already and yet each time we've had to create one, we have found it an equally challenging problem. We are confident that the job directory and the minion directory for Minyawns.com is our best work yet.


Good use of color cues and clear status + action values have ensured that there are lots of applications to jobs.
