Ajency HR

 

People Manager

Ajency.in has grown quickly from a room in the house to an office with 20+ people and continues to grow at a healthy pace. We have new team members joining every second week and this pace will continue for the next few months. So while it was all small and manageable before, we now need help in doing a better job of running the office. Call it a Human Resources role if you may. On a typical day you can expect to do some of the following:


  • 1Manage the office administration and upkeep. This would involve managing two house keeping staff.
  • 2Our typical work day can be stressful and hectic. It helps to have some excitement and fun sprinkled in. This can be our weekly 'Fun Wednesday' or the 'Show and Tell Thursday'. Also on most days we have a "Tasty Evening" where the team gets together over a cup of coffee/ tea and some snacks. You will be responsible for keeping the team positive and looking forward to the day at work.
  • 3Recruitment is a big focus area for us. We will need all the help you can offer on this. This will include reaching out to candidates over phone and email, scheduling interviews, coordinating with them etc.
  • 4Other regular Human Resource responsibilities like maintaining team records, payroll, documentation etc.

 

You will be a great fit if you have:

While we are not looking for industry veterans, a minimum professional experience of 3 years is required (ideal if you have worked for 5+ years). We are not particular about the industry and work profile you may have had. We are looking for a generalist and hence people from sectors that have nothing to do with design and technology are also welcome to apply. Of course, having relevant experience carries weight in our selection process.

Strong written and spoken English is a prerequisite. Soft skills like pleasant personality, meticulous, organized and initiative are what we look for beyond what the resume can tell.
