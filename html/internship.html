Diploma Projects and Internships at Ajency.in

We don't have interns working on client projects. However, we realise that an internship or diploma project allows us to push the envelope. It allows us to experiment with internal ideas that are constantly brewing or with pro bono work that we take up for Not for Profits. We are looking for design, technology and marketing students who wish to join us for a minimum of 8 week projects.

Graphic Design Internship

Project brief 1: There are identity projects that look good and then there are identity projects that make you sit up and notice. Brochures that re-imagine how shape and paper can be used to communicate a message. Visiting cards that question the shape, size and form of what is the norm. We want to re-imagine (yes we used in the last sentence) everything about a company's identity. And we want to do it for ourselves. You get a free hand to build our visual, print and office space identity. Digital can be included though print and space will be the focus of our project. Pre and post research is another important aspect of the design and identity project.

Project brief 2: We are fortunate to be based out of Goa. It is amazing and amusing how inspite of being such a popular tourist place it does not have a well done 'guide'for a visitor. There is plenty of content out there but poorly packaged and presented. A clutter breaking design and approach to a local tour guide is easier said than done. And that is where you come in. We are thinking a clutter breaking print map/guide book, mobile app, web site. We even have a domain for it "HandpickedGoa.com"

 

UI/UX Design + Information design

Project brief 1: We work with a not for profit called VideoVolunteers . You can read more about them on their website Videovolunteers(work in progress site). They are now beginning to struggle with managing their spread community correspondents and want to explore how the entire system can be managed. So think of the process of onboarding a semi literate villager in Jharkhand to them being able to send videos and stories to the VV team and it going up on the site. Post that the follow up, the updates and the whole work flow. Could be a challenging project and one that gets implemented as well. Essentially a software for customized work flow management and process management; which works on web and mobile.
