What is it like to work at Ajency.in?

Goa is many things to many people. So while it's sunny beaches and relaxed atmosphere are a big draw, Ajency.in is based out of Goa for the quality of life it offers. With clean cities, short commutes, pleasant and spacious work places, we believe it is ideal for creative work as well as software development.

Work desks with a view, ergonomic keyboards, ample of empty space with desks spread out, laptops with good internet connection (macs for those can't do without it) are things we take for granted. It's not only lip service when we talk about work life balance. We actively discourage working late hours and would rather have you spend the extra effort building new skills. We work 9.30 a.m to 6.30 p.m, five days a week. For those who prefer a late start, we even have team members who start their day at 12.00 noon.

Growth Opportunities

At Ajency.in, we have grown from two people husband-wife team to now a 20 member strong company. Needless to say, this presented a lot of growth opportunities for everyone involved. In a year where most companies were giving 10-20% hikes, we have had almost everyone see a 50-100% jump in their salary. The initial team members have now become part of the core group, run their own teams and are members of our profit sharing program.

We intend to double in size and revenue over the next 6 months and this, once again, presents an opportunity for everyone involved to grow in responsibility and remuneration.

What kind of people have done well at Ajency.in All kinds :) We have had people with next to no experience and some with 5+ years experience join us, and each has found a role for themselves. The qualities we look for beyond the functional skills, are the hunger to learn and the ambition to do well. We don't have designations and everyone is cooperative and friendly. No egos and no need to play politics.

 

Web Developer

 

Programmers and web developers

We have a fairly exciting technology stack and we are constantly upgrading it. Lately we have been storing a lot of stuff as JSON in the database, and hence now exploring postgres or MongoDB. Though whether we will really make the switch away from MySQL is something we don't know.

We are most excited about Javascript, and how it has become the go to language for everything from mobile apps to server side scripting. We make extensive use of MVC frameworks like Backbone.js and are big believers in cross platform development for mobile using Titanium.

We use Wordpress extensively as our CMS and as the base for user management, session management and everything else.

If PHP and Javasscript are technologies that you like working with, then you will be at home with Ajency.in.

Designer

 

Designers and front end developers

Our lead designer has no formal education in design. He is a self-taught designer who is equally comfortable designing in Photoshop or designing straight in the browser. He can also write PHP and javascript. Our other design lead is a film and communications graduate. Essentially, while formal education in design helps, we are not looking at your degree while interviewing you.

If design, web + mobile design in particular, excites you and you have some body of work to show, then we want to hear from you. Send us your print or digital portfolio and we will get the conversation started.

The only caveat: we are an application heavy team and those who prefer a more theoretical or research oriented approach might not enjoy it much.
