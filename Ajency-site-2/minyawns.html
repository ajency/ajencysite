Minyawns.com
A jobs listing site where employers can hire University students for small tasks.

There are dozens of small jobs sites including the market leaders like TaskRabbit.com and Fanchyhands.com. Minyawns.com (a take on the word Minions) differentiates itself by focusing on University students only. Started by a University of Washington student it has started with the Seattle region and is seeing a healthy adoption.

Landing page - Conversions

While designing the home page we had a clear brief of getting as many conversions (signups) as possible.

A mix of A/B testing and good design practices ensured a healthy sign up success for the home page. Being a responsive site even the mobile signups were a significant percentage.

 

 

Browse Jobs & Minions

It's funny how a directory or listing of jobs and employees has been done a few hundred thousand times already and yet each time we have had to create one we have found it equally challenging problem. We are confident that by far the job directory and the minion directory for Minyawns.com is our best work yet.


Good use of color cues and clear status + Action values have ensured that there are lots of applications to jobs.
