Impruw: A drag and drop web application to build websites.

Impruw is our most ambitious product development project till now. Being built for the travel accommodation industry in Norway and London the service is a drag and drop website builder. The service will integrate 3 aspects of the online presence for a hotel or B&B. A modern and responsive website, a booking engine for rooms and marketing tools for the hotel owner to engage with its site visitors and room guests.

The site is work in progress and is expected to start trial runs in March 2014.

how simple it makes it to create a website.

 

 

Outsourced Product Development

We keep talking about how we are more than just designers or developers. Impruw is a perfect example of the same. The only brief the founders came to us with is "To build a DIY (Do It Yourself) website tool for hotels in Norway and London". We crystallized that brief into a feature document and a phased approach.

 

 

Flat and Responsive

We did some dipsticks in the target market of Norway and keeping with the culture there they preferred a very understated and minimal look. Different design guides were circulated among the target audience and keeping with current trends the flat UI implementation was preferred by the majority.

 

 

Design

More than just UI and UX lot of opportunity for our designers to flex their fingers. Logo design, creating a library of templates for the website builder. We have had a lot of fun (and continue to have) working on the product.

 

 

Internationalization

If you have ever built a web app that works in more than one language you know how painful it is. Impruw is built to work on Norwegian and English from the day 0.
